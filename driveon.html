The DriveOn Legacy

Since 2003, Vijay Industries has been manufacturing automotive accessories under the DRIVEON brand. What began as a dedicated endeavor to improve acoustic performance in passenger vehicles has evolved into a comprehensive range of premium interior accessories trusted by audiophiles and car enthusiasts across India.

We focus on the intersection of acoustic design, ergonomic comfort, and precise mechanical engineering. By manufacturing vehicle-specific solutions, we ensure a seamless, factory-fit appearance that preserves and enhances your car's natural interior lines.
