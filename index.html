Methodology

Manufacturing Process

01

Design & Engineering Review

We analyze drawings and CAD models, reviewing structural requirements, weld configurations, and tolerances to ensure accurate pre-fabrication planning.

02

Material Engineering

Sourcing premium structural steel plates, sections, and welding consumables. Materials undergo chemical and mechanical testing to guarantee tensile strength.

03

Precision Fabrication

Cutting, shearing, plate bending, and high-precision welding execute the core fabrication block, strictly adhering to certified standards.

04

Surface Treatment & Finishing

Blasting and coating application protect the fabrications from corrosion, ensuring long-term surface durability for demanding environments.

05

Rigorous Inspection & Delivery

Non-destructive weld testing, dimensional inspection, and assembly trials precede logistics dispatch and secure site delivery.
